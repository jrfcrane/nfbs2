Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

North Florida Building Solutions ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when you visit northfloridabuildingsolutions.com (the "Site") or do business with us.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide. When you request a quote, fill out a contact or rent-to-own form, place a deposit, or otherwise contact us, we may collect your name, phone number, email address, mailing or delivery address, and details about the building or service you are interested in.

Payment information. When you place a deposit or payment online, payment is processed by our third-party payment processor, PayPal. We do not collect or store your full credit card or bank account numbers on our servers.

Information collected automatically. Like most websites, we automatically collect certain technical information when you visit the Site, such as your IP address, browser type, device information, pages viewed, and the dates and times of your visits. This may be collected through cookies and similar technologies.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide quotes, estimates, and customer service;
  • To process orders, deposits, payments, and rent-to-own applications;
  • To schedule and complete delivery, installation, or related services;
  • To improve and maintain the Site and our products and services;
  • To send you information about your order or, where permitted, about promotions and offers;
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with:

  • Service providers who help us operate our business, such as our payment processor (PayPal), website hosting provider, and mapping services (Google Maps);
  • Manufacturers, dealers, and financing or rent-to-own partners as needed to fulfill your order or application;
  • Legal and safety purposes, when required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our customers or business.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The Site may use cookies and similar technologies to operate properly, remember your preferences, and understand how visitors use the Site. You can set your browser to refuse cookies, but some features of the Site may not function correctly as a result.

5. Third-Party Links and Services

The Site may contain links to third-party websites and services (for example, building configurators, financing partners, or payment pages). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information against loss, misuse, and unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

The Site is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

8. Your Choices and Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, or ask us to stop sending you promotional messages, by contacting us using the information below. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under applicable privacy laws.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of the Site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
