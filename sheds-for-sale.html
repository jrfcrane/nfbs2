Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying a shed direct.

How does rent-to-own work with sheds?
It's a flexible payment plan that lets you work toward ownership with simple monthly payments. Best of all, your credit score isn't a factor — we don't require a credit check to get started. Everyone is approved.
How much are your sheds?
Pricing is determined by the specific model, dimensions, and customization options you choose.
What's the monthly payment on my shed?
Your monthly payment is based on the model and dimensions of the unit you choose. Once you select your shed and any additional features, we will provide a comprehensive breakdown of your monthly payment options before you finalize your agreement. Our website also lists monthly prices on the sheds we advertise.
How soon can a shed be delivered?
Most sheds are delivered within 1–2 business days. Delivery windows may vary slightly based on your specific location and our current inventory.
How much is shed delivery?
Standard delivery is included in the purchase price of your shed. We believe in transparent pricing, so the cost of transporting the unit to your property is already covered.
Are sheds customizable?
Yes! While our sheds are pre-fabricated for quality, we offer numerous aesthetic and functional upgrades, including color schemes, roofing materials, and door placements.
Does a shed require a permit?
While we do not require a permit to initiate your order, permit requirements vary significantly by location. We strongly recommend consulting your local county or municipal planning department to clarify building codes and zoning ordinances for your specific address.
